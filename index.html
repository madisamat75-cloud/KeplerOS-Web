KeplerOS

KeplerOS is a performance-oriented, minimalist operating system built upon the foundation of Arch Linux. Named after Johannes Kepler, whose laws of planetary motion revolutionized our understanding of the universe, KeplerOS is designed to bring that same mathematical precision and efficiency to your computing environment.

The Philosophy

In an era of bloated operating systems and unnecessary background processes, KeplerOS returns to the core principles of computing: speed, efficiency, and absolute control. By stripping away the "garbage" that slows down modern systems, we’ve created a workspace where every CPU cycle and every megabyte of RAM is dedicated to your workflow.

Key Features

  • Arch-Based Core: Benefit from the power and flexibility of the Arch Linux ecosystem.
  • Hardware-Accelerated: Optimized for modern high-end hardware, specifically NVIDIA RTX 50-series GPUs.
  • Hyprland Compositor: Ultra-fluid Wayland compositor for smooth motion and productivity.

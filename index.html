KeplerOS

KeplerOS is a performance-oriented, minimalist operating system built upon the foundation of Arch Linux. Named after Johannes Kepler, whose laws of planetary motion revolutionized our understanding of the universe, KeplerOS is designed to bring that same mathematical precision and efficiency to your computing environment. The Philosophy In an era of bloated operating systems and unnecessary background processes, KeplerOS returns to the core principles of computing: speed, efficiency, and absolute control. By stripping away the "garbage" that slows down modern systems, we’ve created a workspace where every CPU cycle and every megabyte of RAM is dedicated to your workflow—whether that’s compiling custom kernels, developing software, or pushing your hardware to its absolute limit. Key Features Arch-Based Core: Benefit from the power and flexibility of the Arch Linux ecosystem, ensuring you always have the latest kernel and software updates. Hardware-Accelerated Workflow: Out-of-the-box support for modern high-end hardware, specifically optimized for NVIDIA RTX 50-series GPUs. Hyprland Compositor: A stunning, ultra-fluid Wayland compositor that provides desktop motion as smooth as planetary orbits, perfectly balanced between aesthetics and productivity. Dev-Ready Environment: Pre-configured tools for C, C++, and system-level development. From compilers to emulation environments like QEMU, everything is ready to go the moment you boot. Minimalist Design: No bloatware. No hidden telemetry. Just a clean, high-performance interface that lets you focus on what you're building.
